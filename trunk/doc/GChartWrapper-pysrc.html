 17  """ 
 18  GChartWrapper - Google Chart API Wrapper 
 19   
 20  The wrapper can render the URL of the Google chart based on your parameters. 
 21  With the chart you can render an HTML img tag to insert into webpages on the fly,  
 22  show it directly in a webbrowser, or save the chart PNG to disk. 
 23   
 24  Example 
 25   
 26      >>> G = GChart('lc',['simpleisbetterthancomplexcomplexisbetterthancomplicated']) 
 27      >>> G.title('The Zen of Python','00cc00',36) 
 28      >>> G.color('00cc00') 
 29      >>> str(G) 
 30      '''http://chart.apis.google.com/chart? 
 31          chd=s:simpleisbetterthancomplexcomplexisbetterthancomplicated 
 32          &chco=00cc00 
 33          &chts=00cc00,36 
 34          &chs=300x150 
 35          &cht=lc 
 36          &chtt=The+Zen+of+Python'''
