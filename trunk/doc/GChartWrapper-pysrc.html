 12  """ 
 13  GChartWrapper - Google Chart API Wrapper 
 14   
 15  The wrapper can render the URL of the Google chart based on your parameters. 
 16  With the chart you can render an HTML img tag to insert into webpages on the fly,  
 17  show it directly in a webbrowser, or save the chart PNG to disk. 
 18   
 19  Example 
 20   
 21      >>> G = GChart('lc',['simpleisbetterthancomplexcomplexisbetterthancomplicated']) 
 22      >>> G.title('The Zen of Python','00cc00',36) 
 23      >>> G.color('00cc00') 
 24      >>> str(G) 
 25      '''http://chart.apis.google.com/chart? 
 26          chd=s:simpleisbetterthancomplexcomplexisbetterthancomplicated 
 27          &chco=00cc00 
 28          &chts=00cc00,36 
 29          &chs=300x150 
 30          &cht=lc 
 31          &chtt=The+Zen+of+Python''' 
 32   
 33  See tests.py for unit test and other examples 
 34  """
