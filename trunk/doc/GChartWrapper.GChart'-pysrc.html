 12  """ 
 13  GChartWrapper - Google Chart API Wrapper 
 14   
 15  The wrapper can render the URL of the Google chart based on your parameters. 
 16  With the chart you can render an HTML img tag to insert into webpages on the fly, 
 17  show it directly in a webbrowser, or save the chart PNG to disk. New versions 
 18  can generate PIL PngImage instances. 
 19   
 20  Example 
 21   
 22      >>> G = GChart('lc',['simpleisbetterthancomplexcomplexisbetterthancomplicated']) 
 23      >>> G.title('The Zen of Python','00cc00',36) 
 24      >>> G.color('00cc00') 
 25      >>> G 
 26      '''http://chart.apis.google.com/chart? 
 27          chd=s:simpleisbetterthancomplexcomplexisbetterthancomplicated 
 28          &chco=00cc00 
 29          &chts=00cc00,36 
 30          &chs=300x150 
 31          &cht=lc 
 32          &chtt=The+Zen+of+Python''' 
 33      >>> G.image() # PIL instance 
 34      <PngImagePlugin.PngImageFile instance at 0xb79fe2ac> 
 35      >>> G.show() # Webbrowser open 
 36      True 
 37      >>> G.save('tmp.png') # Save to disk 
 38      'tmp.png' 
 39   
 40  See tests.py for unit test and other examples 
 41  """
